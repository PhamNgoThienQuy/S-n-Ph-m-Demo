Dell Inspiron 15 is a Windows 10 Home laptop with a 15.60-inch display that has a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels. It is powered by a Core i5 processor and it comes with 8GB of RAM. The Dell Inspiron 15 packs 1TB of HDD storage.
